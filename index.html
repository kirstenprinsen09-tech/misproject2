Vinyl Collecting

(A photo of my current vinyl collection)

I love collecting vinyl records — not just for the music, but for everything that comes with it. The colors, the artwork, the way each pressing feels a little different. Some albums I own five times over, just to get every color variant… which probably sounds a little crazy, but it's just so much fun. There's something satisfying about seeing them all lined up, knowing each one has its own story or memory attached. It's a hobby that mixes creativity, nostalgia, and a bit of joyful obsession.
