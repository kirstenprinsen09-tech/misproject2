Why I Chose UMD

I chose the University of Minnesota Duluth because it's close to home, in a beautiful part of the state, and has a great business school. After moving around a lot growing up, I was looking for a place that felt steady and offered strong academic opportunities — UMD checked all the boxes.

What I Love About Duluth

One of my favorite things to do is spend time by Lake Superior, but no matter where you are in Duluth, there's always something beautiful to see — whether it's the changing colors of the trees, the view from the hilltop, or the quiet moments by the water. That's what I love about this place: it's peaceful, scenic, and full of little spots that make you pause and appreciate where you are. Duluth's natural beauty makes it a great place to study, explore, and recharge, especially when you need a break from campus life. It's the kind of environment that helps you stay focused while still feeling inspired.

Campus Highlights

Being involved in Women in Business, BizDog Council, and Chi Alpha has helped me build friendships and grow as a leader. Each organization has given me a different kind of community — professional, creative, and spiritual. UMD has also sparked my interest in studying abroad, thanks to how popular and accessible those opportunities are here.
